It was a crisp spring morning in the park, and the world felt new again. Gentle rays of sunlight filtered through the budding leaves, casting playful patterns on the winding path.

![Alba al parco](/artcont/test1.webp)

Birds chirped overhead, and a soft breeze carried the scent of fresh grass. You could almost hear the earth waking up.

- I saw ducks gliding across the pond  
- I smelled the first blossoms of the season  
- I felt the warmth of the sun after a long winter  

Le emozioni si mescolavano: **meraviglia**, _serenità_ e un pizzico di **_euforia_**.
